Easy Vanilla Ice Cream

Description

This easy ice cream recipe requires no cooking and no eggs. Half-and-half and cream team up with sugar to create a simple, old-fashioned vanilla ice cream that tastes great

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts half-and-half
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • ½ pint heavy cream
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 pinch salt

Steps

  1. Combine half-and-half, sugar, cream, vanilla, and salt in the freezer container of an ice cream maker.
  2. Freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes.
  3. Transfer to an airtight container and freeze until firm, about 4 hours.
